What We Check

Comprehensive Website Health Monitoring

Every report covers six critical areas that affect your site's reliability, security, and search ranking.

SSL Certificate

Monitor certificate expiry dates, validate the chain of trust, and verify protocol version. Get alerted weeks before your SSL expires.

Performance

Track response times, measure availability, and identify slow endpoints. Know exactly how fast your site loads for real users.

Security Headers

Check for Content-Security-Policy, HSTS, X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, and more. Harden your site against common attacks.

SEO Basics

Validate title tags, meta descriptions, canonical URLs, and Open Graph tags. Make sure search engines and social platforms see your site correctly.

DNS Health

Verify DNS record resolution, check propagation, and identify misconfigurations that could cause downtime or email delivery issues.

Technology Stack

Detect web server, framework, CDN, and CMS. Understand what powers your site and get recommendations for your specific stack.
