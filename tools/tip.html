Tipping Guidelines

  • 15% — Standard for adequate service
  • 18% — Good service (most common default)
  • 20% — Great service
  • 25%+ — Exceptional service

Tips are typically calculated on the pre-tax bill amount. In the US, servers often rely on tips as a significant part of their income. When splitting a bill, make sure the total tip still reflects the quality of service you received.
