How Salary Conversions Work

Salary conversions depend on how many hours you work per week and how many weeks per year you work. Standard assumptions are 40 hours/week and 52 weeks/year (2,080 hours total).

  • Annual to Hourly: Annual Salary / (Hours per Week x Weeks per Year)
  • Hourly to Annual: Hourly Rate x Hours per Week x Weeks per Year
  • Monthly: Annual Salary / 12
  • Bi-Weekly: Annual Salary / 26
  • Weekly: Annual Salary / 52
  • Daily: Weekly / 5 (assumes 5-day work week)

If you receive paid vacation, use 52 weeks. If unpaid, subtract your vacation weeks (e.g., 50 weeks for 2 weeks unpaid vacation).
